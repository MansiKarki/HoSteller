1. Hostel Overview

The hostel is dedicated to promoting a safe, comfortable, and academically conducive environment for all residents, central to the campus experience.

  • Capacity: Total of 500 beds across two main blocks (East and West).
  • Type: Separate **Boys' and Girls' blocks** (E-Block for Boys, W-Block for Girls).
  • Location: Located just 5 minutes walk from the main academic buildings, offering convenient access.
  • Accommodation: Both **AC and Non-AC options** available for shared rooms.
  • Management: Overseen by a committee comprising the Chief Warden, Asst. Warden, and resident faculty advisors.

2. Room Information

Rooms are designed for maximum student comfort and utility, ensuring a great space for both rest and study.

  • Room Types: Double and Triple sharing rooms available. Single rooms are allocated based on seniority/medical needs.
  • Furnishings Provided: Individual bed frame, spring mattress, study table, ergonomic chair, spacious cupboard (with lock), fan, and LED lighting.
  • Allotment Process: Rooms are generally assigned based on **course year and seniority**. Specific requests are accommodated based on availability.
  • Change Policy: Room change requests must be submitted through the student portal within the first two weeks of the semester, subject to warden approval.
  • Housekeeping: Common area cleaning is daily. Room interiors are cleaned by staff **three times per week**.
  • Laundry: Professional paid laundry service is available with drop-off/pickup points on the ground floor of each block.

3. Facilities & Common Areas

Beyond the rooms, residents have access to various resources to support their academic and personal lives.

  • Wi-Fi Access: High-speed 24x7 Wi-Fi is available in all rooms and common areas.
  • Water Supply: 24x7 filtered water supply. RO water purifiers and coolers are installed on every floor.
  • Study Halls: Dedicated quiet reading rooms are available in both blocks for late-night studying.
  • Recreation: Common rooms feature a TV lounge, board games, and a table tennis setup.
  • Fitness: A common mini-gym and yoga hall are available in the central facility block.
  • Electricity: 24x7 electricity with backup generators for uninterrupted supply.

4. Safety & Security

Security and resident safety is the highest priority, with strict protocols in place 24 hours a day.

  • Security Coverage: 24x7 trained security guards and extensive **CCTV coverage** throughout the premises.
  • Entry System: Entry and exit require **ID card validation and biometric scan**.
  • Warden Contacts: Resident Tutors (RAs) are available on each floor for immediate assistance.
  • Curfew: Mandatory **night attendance** is taken at 10:30 PM. Curfew hours apply strictly.
  • Fire Safety: All blocks are equipped with fire alarms, extinguishers on every floor, and clearly posted evacuation plans.
  • Visitors: Visitors are only permitted in the designated reception area during specified visiting hours (4 PM - 7 PM). ID verification is mandatory.
Emergency Security Helpline: **+91 99999 00000 (24x7)**

5. Important Dates & Stay Process

Key dates and procedural timelines for the upcoming academic year and stay renewal processes.

  • Hostel Admission: Applications open from **June 1st to June 20th**.
  • Room Allotment Schedule: Final list announced on **July 5th** on the official notice board.
  • Check-in Timeline: Mandatory check-in window is **July 25th - July 30th**.
  • Renewal Procedure: Renewal for the next year begins in April; notification will be sent via email.
  • Late Arrival Policy: Must inform the warden in advance via email if arrival is expected after the official check-in date.

6. Fee & Payment Information

All fees are payable per semester via the online portal. Please adhere to the deadlines to avoid penalties.

  • Fee Breakdown: Room Rent (varies by AC/Non-AC), Security Deposit (refundable), and Common Facility Charges.
  • Refund Policy: Security deposit is fully refundable upon check-out. Room rent is non-refundable after the first 30 days of the semester.
  • Payment Modes: Online payment via Credit/Debit card, UPI, or Net Banking is preferred.
  • Reminders: Automated fee due reminders are sent 10 days before the deadline. A late fee penalty applies after the due date.

7. Student Life & Support

We believe in holistic development and offer various support systems to help students thrive.

  • Mentorship: Senior students are assigned as mentors (buddy system) to guide new residents.
  • Events: Regular hostel events, cultural evenings, and intramural competitions are organized by the Student Committee.
  • Counseling: Access to confidential, on-campus counseling and mental health support services is available.
  • Grievance: A digital **Complaint Redressal Form** is available for submitting any issues anonymously or formally.
  • Lost & Found: Services managed at the front desk of the central facility block.

8. For Parents

Dedicated information to assure parents of their child's well-being and safety while residing at the hostel.

  • Warden Contact: Direct contact numbers for the Chief Warden are provided upon admission.
  • Emergency Helpline: 24x7 emergency contacts available for parents.
  • Visiting Hours: Parents may visit their wards during the designated visiting hours only (4 PM - 7 PM).
  • Stay Arrangements: Dedicated guest house facility is available for parents visiting from out of town (booking required).
  • Safety Assurance: We maintain strict monitoring and discipline systems to ensure a focused and safe environment.
