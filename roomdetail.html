1. Hostel Overview

The hostel promotes a safe, comfortable, and academically supportive environment for residents.

  • Capacity: 500 beds across two main blocks.
  • Type: Separate Boys' and Girls' blocks.
  • Location: 5 minutes walk from academic buildings.
  • Accommodation: AC and Non-AC rooms available.

2. Room Information

Rooms are designed for comfort and utility, suitable for rest and study.

  • Room Types: Double and triple sharing rooms.
  • Furnishings: Bed, mattress, table, chair, cupboard, fan, LED light.

3. Facilities & Common Areas

  • Wi-Fi and 24-hour power backup.
  • Common TV and recreation rooms.
  • Indoor games and reading lounges.
  • Clean dining hall with nutritious food.

4. Safety & Security

  • 24x7 security staff and CCTV surveillance.
  • Access control for visitors.
  • Emergency medical support available.

5. Important Dates & Process

  • Application Start: June 1
  • Room Allotment: July 10
  • Check-in: From July 20

6. Fee & Payment Information

  • Room Fee: ₹45,000 (per semester)
  • Mess Fee: ₹15,000 (per semester)
  • Security Deposit: ₹5,000 (refundable)

7. Student Life & Support

We provide student counseling, wellness programs, and a mentorship system to support academic and personal growth.

8. For Parents

Parents can reach the warden’s office for updates, visit requests, or emergencies anytime during office hours.
