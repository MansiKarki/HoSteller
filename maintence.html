Maintenance Request Guide

1. Electrical Maintenance 2. Plumbing & Water Supply 3. Furniture Repair 4. Room & Building Structural 5. Bathroom Maintenance 6. Cleaning & Housekeeping 7. Internet & Wi-Fi Support 8. Common Area Maintenance

1. Electrical Maintenance

  • Fan not working or making noise
  • Tube light / LED / bulb replacement
  • Power fluctuations inside the room
  • Switchboard repair
  • Broken plug sockets
  • Room power failure
  • Short circuit or burning smell
  • Charging points not working
  • AC/Heater electrical issues (if available)
  • Corridor or bathroom light problems
  • Loose or exposed wiring
  • Wi-Fi router power issues

2. Plumbing & Water Supply

  • Tap leakage
  • Pipe leakage (room/bathroom)
  • No water supply
  • Low water pressure
  • Blocked washbasin
  • Clogged toilet
  • Shower not working
  • Flush not working
  • Water tank overflow
  • Constant dripping sound
  • Hot water supply problems (if available)
  • Bathroom floor water drainage issues

3. Furniture Repair

  • Broken bed
  • Bed frame noise or movement
  • Damaged study table
  • Loose or broken chairs
  • Cupboard or locker not closing properly
  • Broken cupboard shelves
  • Drawer jammed or not sliding smoothly
  • Pinboard or wall hooks installation
  • Broken mirror inside cupboard
  • Shoe rack repair

4. Room & Building Structural

  • Wall cracks
  • Paint peeling
  • Room dampness or moisture
  • Ceiling plaster falling
  • Broken or loose tiles
  • Window glass cracked
  • Window mesh damaged
  • Door lock or handle repair
  • Door alignment issues
  • Loose or broken door hinges
  • Balcony railing issues
  • Curtain rod fixing

5. Bathroom Maintenance

  • Broken bucket or mug replacement (if provided by hostel)
  • Bathroom ventilation fan not working
  • Showerhead replacement
  • Broken soap stand or bathroom rack
  • Drain blockage
  • Water leakage from below the toilet
  • Loose bathroom taps

6. Cleaning & Housekeeping

  • Extra room cleaning
  • Bathroom cleaning
  • Dustbin clearance
  • Deep cleaning after any accident or spillage
  • Weekly or monthly scheduled cleaning
  • Cleaning after water leakage incidents
  • Removal of garbage piles
  • Window or curtain cleaning (if provided)

7. Internet & Wi-Fi Support

  • Wi-Fi not working in room
  • Slow internet speed
  • Router not working
  • Repeated disconnection
  • Wi-Fi login issues
  • Poor signal strength
  • Internet not working on one side of the floor

8. Common Area Maintenance

  • Study room lights and fans
  • Lounge/TV room equipment
  • Dining hall and mess area repairs
  • Common bathroom issues
  • Lift or escalator issues (if available)
  • Staircase lights
  • Garden or lawn maintenance
  • Sports equipment repair
